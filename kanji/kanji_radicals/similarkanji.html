Similar-Looking Kanji Quiz


This casual quiz will test your ability to discern between the many, many pairs and groups of kanji that look very similar to each other. For each group, examine closely and decide which kanji represents the meaning given. For bonus points, try to identify the other kanji in each group.

Each group gets more difficult as you progress.
